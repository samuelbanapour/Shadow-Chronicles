Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Dark Sovereign RPG application ("the App"), available on the web and Android via Google Play.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The App does not require account creation, login, or any form of registration.

Local Storage

The App saves your game progress (character name, stats, story choices, and inventory) locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics tools, tracking pixels, cookies, or advertising identifiers. We do not track your usage in any way.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, social networks, or external APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any data from users of any age. Because no data is collected at all, there is no special handling required for users under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer at the email address listed on the Google Play Store listing.

Dark Sovereign RPG — All game data is stored locally on your device only.
