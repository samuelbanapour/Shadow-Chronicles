Privacy Policy

Dark Sovereign RPG · Last updated 11 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Game Dev Solo (“we”, “us”) handles information in connection with the game Dark Sovereign RPG (the “Game”). By installing or playing the Game, you agree to this policy.

Information we and our partners collect

  • Name & basic account info. If you choose to sign in with your Google account or Google Play Games, we receive your name and basic profile information from Google to identify you and save achievements.
  • Approximate location. Our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may infer your general, city-level location from your IP address to serve relevant ads. We do not collect precise/GPS location.
  • Device or other identifiers. Google AdMob collects your device’s advertising identifier and similar device identifiers to serve, limit, and measure ads.
  • App activity (app interactions). We and our partners collect in-app actions — such as screens viewed and ad interactions — for analytics and advertising.
  • Crash logs & diagnostics. We collect crash reports and performance/diagnostic data to keep the Game stable and fix problems.
  • Feedback you choose to send. If you use the in-game feedback form, we receive your message and basic context about where you were in the Game (such as the chapter or scene).
  • On-device save data. Your game progress is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

How the information is used

  • Advertising: approximate location, device identifiers, and app interactions are used to display and measure ads, which fund the Game.
  • App functionality & account: your name/account info enables sign-in and achievements.
  • Analytics & improvement: app interactions, crash logs, and diagnostics help us understand usage and improve stability.
  • Support: feedback you send is used to review and improve the Game.

Sharing & third-party services

The Game uses third-party services that process some of this data under their own privacy policies. In particular, advertising data (approximate location, advertising/device identifiers, and ad interactions) is shared with Google for advertising and measurement.

  • Google AdMob, Google Sign-In & Google Play services — see the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from apps that use its services.

We do not sell your personal information for money.

Ads and your choices

You can control ad personalization and your advertising ID on your device: on Android, open Settings → Google → Ads to opt out of personalized ads or to reset/delete your advertising ID. Users in the EEA/UK are asked for consent before personalized ads are shown, where required.

Children

Dark Sovereign RPG contains mature, dark-fantasy themes and is intended for mature audiences. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Data retention & security

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above, and we use reasonable measures to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Advertising identifiers can be reset or deleted by you at any time using your device settings.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example, under the EU/UK GDPR or the California CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information and to opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” of personal information for targeted advertising. You can use the device-level ad choices described above, or contact us using the details below to make a request.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the most recent version.
